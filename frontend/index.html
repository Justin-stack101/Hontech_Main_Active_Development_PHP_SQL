Knowledge Base & FAQ

Frequently asked questions and guides

How do I add a new vehicle to the Queue?

Go to the "Intake" view in the navigation, fill in the customer name, license plate, vehicle brand/model, and select the service. Once submitted, it automatically appears in the live queue dashboard.

How can I download a PDF or Word report?

In the "Dashboard" overview screen, there are dedicated export buttons at the top right of the screen. Click on "PDF" or "Word" to instantly download the generated report documents.

How do I change color themes?

Click your user profile avatar in the upper right header to expand the user dropdown menu. Under "Switch Color Theme", click on the colored circles (Teal, Blue, Purple, Orange, Red) to change colors.
